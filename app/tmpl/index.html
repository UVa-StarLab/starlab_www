  • Innovation & Progress

    Our projects involve the security of cyber-physical systems and relation to creating safer experiences for autonomous driving vehicles. At UVA StarLab we strive for our research to create safer driving experiences for the next generation.

  • Our Team & Experience

    Our team's experience ranges broadly from cyber-physical systems research, embedded systems, human-automation interaction and software security to robotics and software architecture.
